About Us


Dakshta Foundation is a non profit organization working with an aim to make India disease-free and healthy. We have a team of more than 100 volunteers, who organise health camps and free-medicine drives in various underprivileged areas of Delhi/ NCR.

Our Causes

Healthcare for all

Dakshta Foundation works on this mission and is always ready to do everything possible to make our country healthy and fit.

Women empowerment

Dakshta Foundation proclaims solidarity and stands firm with women in need or danger. We also support other organizations who work in this direction.

Food for all

Dakshta Foundation organizes free food stalls to help people from under privileged sections of the society.
